Executive Summary

Net4Talents – The Reputation Layer of the Internet

The internet has made knowledge universally accessible.

Yet finding the right knowledge has become increasingly difficult.

Every day, millions of videos, articles, repositories, courses, and opinions compete for our attention. Search engines help us find information. Social media helps us discover content. But neither helps us reliably answer a more important question:

What is actually worth my time?

Net4Talents is building the Reputation Layer of the Internet.

Our mission is to help people discover trusted knowledge, credible experts, valuable learning resources, and promising ideas through the collective intelligence of communities.

Unlike traditional platforms that rank content based on popularity, engagement, or advertising, Net4Talents uses reputation, recommendations, coaching activities, and community validation to identify quality and trust.

The platform combines six core capabilities:

Discover

Discover knowledge, people, projects, and ideas that would otherwise remain hidden.

Find

Find the most relevant resources within a specific topic, skill area, or professional domain.

Trusted

Build confidence through community-driven validation and transparent reputation mechanisms.

Reputation

Reward people who contribute value, share knowledge, mentor others, and consistently help the community learn.

Find Experts

Identify practitioners, coaches, mentors, and specialists whose expertise has been validated by others.

Knowledge Discovery

Create a curated knowledge layer across existing platforms such as YouTube, GitHub, blogs, podcasts, and online communities.

The first implementation focuses on creating trusted indexes of content and expertise, allowing members to discover the most valuable resources and the most respected contributors within any area of interest.

Our long-term vision is to become the world’s most trusted platform for knowledge discovery, expert identification, and reputation-based learning.

Google helps people find information.

Net4Talents helps people find what is worth learning and who is worth listening to.
