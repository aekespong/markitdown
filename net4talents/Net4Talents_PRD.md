# Net4Talents

## Product Requirements Document (PRD)

### Vision

Net4Talents is a community-driven knowledge discovery platform.

Its purpose is to help people discover:

- The most valuable knowledge
- The most credible experts
- The most useful learning resources
- The most promising open-source projects

The platform combines reputation, recommendations, coaching, and community feedback to create a trusted map of knowledge and talent.

---

# Problem

The internet contains an overwhelming amount of content.

Every day thousands of:

- YouTube videos
- GitHub repositories
- Articles
- Tutorials
- AI tools

are published.

Finding high-quality content is difficult because existing ranking systems primarily measure popularity.

Popularity is not the same as usefulness.

As a result:

- Valuable content remains hidden.
- Experts are difficult to identify.
- New learners waste time on low-quality material.
- Communities repeatedly solve the same discovery problem.

---

# Solution

Net4Talents creates a trusted knowledge network.

Members discover and recommend:

- YouTube videos
- GitHub repositories
- Articles
- Experts
- Coaches

Recommendations are weighted by the credibility of the member making them.

The platform continuously improves discovery by combining:

- Community recommendations
- Talent Scores
- Coaching activity
- User feedback
- Topic-based tagging

---

# Core Concepts

## Talent

A member of the network.

Talents have:

- Profile
- Expertise areas
- Reputation
- Talent Score
- Coaching history

## Content

Content may include:

- YouTube videos
- GitHub repositories
- Blog posts
- Podcasts
- Learning resources

All content is tagged.

Examples:

- Enterprise Architecture
- AI Agents
- Python
- Cloudflare
- Integration Architecture
- Leadership

## Talent Score

A measure of trust and expertise.

Factors include:

### Community Recommendations

Recommendations from other members.

### Coaching Activities

Helping another member through a coaching session.

### Content Discovery

Identifying valuable content that is later confirmed by the community.

### Community Feedback

Positive ratings from members.

---

# YouTube Index

The YouTube Index is a curated ranking of videos.

Videos are ranked using:

- Community votes
- Talent Score of recommenders
- Topic relevance
- Historical usefulness

The objective is to surface the most valuable videos rather than the most popular videos.

A recommendation from a highly trusted member has greater impact than a recommendation from a new member.

---

# GitHub Index

The GitHub Index identifies valuable repositories.

Repositories are ranked based on:

- Community recommendations
- Quality assessments
- Relevance to specific topics
- Trust level of reviewers

# Coaching

Members can request and offer short coaching sessions.

Typical duration:

- 20 minutes

After completion:

- Both participants provide feedback.
- Talent Scores are updated.
- Trust relationships are strengthened.
